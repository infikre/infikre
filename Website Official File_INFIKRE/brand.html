FAQ

What businesses usually ask before we begin.

Is Infikre Brand only for logo design?

No. Logo design is one part of the identity. The work includes strategy, positioning, visual language, messaging, guidelines, and launch assets.

Can you rebrand an existing business?

Yes. We audit what exists, protect valuable equity, and rebuild the system so the brand feels sharper, clearer, and ready for growth.

Do I receive usable files and guidelines?

Yes. Deliverables are organized for print, digital, social, presentations, and internal use, with clear guidance for consistency.

Can this support startups and personal brands?

Yes. The process scales from personal brands and startups to enterprise identity systems.
